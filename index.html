Seungwoo Han (한승우)

I am a PhD student at Biosignal informatics Lab. at Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology advised by Prof. Toshihisa Tanaka. My research focuses on graph singal processing and graph learning.

I completed a MSc degree from Kwangwoon University under the supervision of Prof. Cheolsoo Park, where I studied arrhythmia classification using deep learning.

I have been actively involved in interdisciplinary research and international collaborations. I am currently working with Prof. Su Man Nam from Cheongju University on wireless sensor networks, and with Dr. Unang Sunarya from Telkom University on biomedical engineering. I am always open to new collaborative opportunities, so please feel free to reach out via email.
