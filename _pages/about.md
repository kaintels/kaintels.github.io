<a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=NWbfyKYAAAAJ&hl">Seungwoo Han</a> is researcher in the field of statistical signal processing.
He currently serves as a Leader at <a href="https://modulabs.co.kr/">MODULABS</a> Multi-agent reinforcement learning environment (Multi-RL Env) LAB.
His interests lie in analyzing biomedical, stock, and graph signals using machine learning techniques. He aims to contribute to monitoring health conditions and developing machine intelligence for humans. He has published over 23 papers in the field of signal processing with AI.
